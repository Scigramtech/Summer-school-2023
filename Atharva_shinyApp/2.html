Location of ores

Iron ores and lithium ores are found in different regions around the world. Here is some information on their yield and locations:

Iron ores:

Iron ores are found in many parts of the world, with the largest reserves located in Australia, Brazil, Russia, China, and India. Other significant iron ore-producing countries include Canada, Ukraine, South Africa, Sweden, and the United States. Iron ore deposits are usually found in sedimentary rocks, such as banded iron formations, and can also be found in igneous rocks such as granite and basalt.
